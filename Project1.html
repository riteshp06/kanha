The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a bread of small scent hound similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound . The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beaging.Possession a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size,good temper,and a lack of inheherited health problems.

Trivia
